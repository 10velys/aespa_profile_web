About æ - æspa

According to their group concept and SMCU lore, each member has an æ-counterpart.

The ae-members were introduced along with the members upon debut and appeared in their music videos and SMCU episodes.

The ae-members’ names are as follows:

ae-Karina (purple hair)

ae-Giselle (shorter blonde hair)

ae-Winter (blonde/pink hair)

ae-NingNing (blonde/purple hair, bangs)

æ - KARINA

Name: ae-KARINA (stylized as æ-karina)

Eye Color: Dark Blue

Hair Color: Dark Purple

She was first revealed during a press conference on October 28, 2020. She can speak both Korean and English.

She met Karina on SYNK and were brought together by NAEVIS.

æ - GISELLE

Name ae-GISELLE (stylized as æ-giselle)

Eye Color: Hazel

Hair Color: Blonde

She was revealed on November 6, 2020 during the SYNK, GISELLE video.

She is the third avatar to be revealed after ae-WINTER.

æ - WINTER

Name: ae-WINTER (stylized as æ-winter)

Eye Color: Light Brown

Hair Color: Pink & Blonde

She was first revealed on November 4, 2020 in the video SYNK, WINTER.

She is the second Avatar to be revealed after æ-KARINA.

She is the only member where the ae-version of herself has the same hair color as her debut color (bleached blonde).

æ - NINGNING

Name: ae-NINGNING (stylized as æ-ningning)

Eye Color: Pink

Hair Color: Platinum Blonde/Purple

She was revealed on November 9, 2020 in the video SYNK, NINGNING

She was the final Avatar to be revealed after æ-GISELLE.
